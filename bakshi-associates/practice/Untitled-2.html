About Us

Who We Are?

Set up in 1987 Bakshi & Associates is a well established law firm. The growth of the firm is attributed to its founder Shri. Sunil Kumar Bakshi whose visionary leadership and hard work has enabled the firm to emerge as a leading Law Firm in India. Having the best lawyers in Faridabad, Palwal, Delhi & Noida for District & Session Courts, High Court & Supreme Court matters. The firm is handling the sophisticated issues from original level to different stages of appellate level in the field of Labour Law, Real Estate, Corporate Laws, Contract and other documents Review, Mergers and Acquisitions, Amalgamations etc. through its Partners and Associate Advocates, Chartered Accountants and Company Secretaries.

It has served with different complex issues in relation to Indian as well as Foreign clients and is capable of providing all kinds of legal support in India as well as outside India. The Firm is well equipped in dealing with the Clients having Family Disputes pertaining to Divorce, Child Custody, Domestic Violence, Maintenance etc.
